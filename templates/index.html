ONLINE NOTEBOOK

The "Online Notebook" is a web platform designed to facilitate collaborative learning and knowledge sharing among students and lecturers. The core feature of the website is a query box where students can post subject-related questions, inviting their peers to share their insights. If no responses are received within two weeks, the lecturer has the option to provide an answer. Additionally, the platform allows users to upload and share subject notes, fostering a collaborative environment for studying and understanding various perspectives on specific topics. The website requires users to sign up and log in, with separate interfaces for both students and lecturers, ensuring a secure and personalized experience. Overall, the Online Notebook aims to enhance the educational experience by promoting engagement, collaboration, and the exchange of knowledge within a user-friendly online community.
